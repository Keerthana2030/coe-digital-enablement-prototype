# Operational Excellence Frameworks

Operational Excellence ensures organizations deliver consistent outcomes through structured improvement systems.

A COE provides:
- governance of improvement initiatives
- standard methodologies
- performance visibility
- training and enablement

Typical maturity levels:
Level 1 – Ad-hoc improvements  
Level 2 – Standard templates and reporting  
Level 3 – Portfolio governance and KPI tracking  
Level 4 – Continuous optimization with automation and AI
